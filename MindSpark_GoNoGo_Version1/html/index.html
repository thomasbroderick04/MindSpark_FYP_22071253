Go/No-Go Test Overview
Overview
The Go/No-Go test is an activity that observes your attention and ability to control your responses

You will see one of two symbols appear on the screen only for a short time

You will be asked to respond when one of the symbols appear, and not respond to the other

To provide your answer, you will have the option to either touch the screen, or use your voice to make a clear audible response There is also an option to have a specific audible tone played with the appearance of each symbol
