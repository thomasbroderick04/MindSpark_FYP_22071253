Go/No-Go Test
Overview
The Go/No-Go test is an activity that
observes your attention and ability to
control your responses. This test typically
takes about 3 minutes to complete
What You'll See
One of two symbols will briefly appear on screen — a target or a distractor symbol
When To Respond
Tap or say "go" as fast as you can when the target symbol appears
When To Hold Back
Stay still and do not respond when the distractor symbol appears
